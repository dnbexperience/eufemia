# UI inspiration

To see how individual elements and components fit together in a typical application, here are some partial and full examples to view for inspiration regarding spacing and composition.

Be aware that the default text styling in Eufemia does not include top and bottom margins or padding, as we see in the alternating form sections below. These spacings must be designed by the designer using the spatial system and incorporated into the design by the developer.
